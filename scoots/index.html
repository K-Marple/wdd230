Welcome to Scoots in Cozumel!

We are your one stop for all vehicle rental needs on the island of Cozumel! Freely explore our island with the help of one of our scooters, ATVs , or Jeeps. You can easily hop between the tourist spots on the western shore, or escape them entirely by zipping over to the east or south with their beautiful shores and inlets.

We offer vehicles from small to large, based on your needs, with prices starting at $20. Our smallest being a 1 person Honda Metropolitan, and the largest, a 4-door Jeep Wrangler. Most of our transportation is open air, with the exception of the 4-door Wrangler which has A/C. Each vehicle comes with unlimited miles and can be returned to either our location by the ferry dock in el Centro or at the Puerta Maya Terminal. For rentals 2 days or longer, we can also deliver the vehicle to your Cozumel hotel or Airbnb.

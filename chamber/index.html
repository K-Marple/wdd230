Clovis Rodeo

One of our biggest and most beloved annual events, the Clovis Rodeo has come each spring for more than 100 years. With several days of rodeo performances with live concerts, dancing, and other activities, there is loads of fun to be had. So saddle up, put on your cowboy boots, and join the excitement at the Clovis Rodeo—where western culture, adrenaline-pumping rodeo events, live music, and dancing come together!

Visitor's Center

Welcome to the Old Town Clovis Visitor's Center! Whether you're a curious traveler passing through or a local seeking hidden gems, our center is your gateway to discovery. Drop by between the hours of 10:00 AM and 5:00 PM for maps and brochures of historic landmarks, local events, walking trails, and must-sees all around Clovis. We also have a lost and found so if you've left anything at one of our parks or landmarks, you miht just find it here. Let us be your compass in this delightful town!

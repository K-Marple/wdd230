Membership Levels

NP Membership

The Non-Profit Membership is specifically designed for non-profit organizations. It provides access to essential features and resources to support their mission-driven work.

This membership level is completely free as a gesture of support for non-profit initiatives.

Bronze Membership

The Bronze Membership offers basic benefits and limited access to additional services. It is suitable for individuals or small businesses looking for essential features. Benefits include a listing in our directory, discounted prices at special events, display brochures or cards in our lobby, access to member-only events, your events on the Chamber calendar, one free ad annually, and an invite to the annual Awards Benefit.

The Bronze Membership costs $40 per month.

Silver Membership

The Silver Membership provides enhanced features, priority support, and access to exclusive content. It is ideal for those seeking a more comprehensive experience. Benefits include all in the Bronze Membership plus an additional free ad annually.

The Silver Membership is priced at $80 per month.

Gold Membership

The Gold Membership is the premium tier, offering all features, personalized assistance, and early access to new features. It is perfect for power users and businesses. Benefits include all of the Silver Membership benefits and an advertising package including a third free annual ad, recognition as Gold Member at events, exclusive access to Chamber hall, and an invite to the annual golf tournament and luncheon.

The Gold Membership comes at $200 per month.
